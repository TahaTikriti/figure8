## Best Practices from Top Governmental/Enterprise Consultancy Websites

### Analyzed Competitors:
- **Deloitte, PwC, Accenture** (Global consultancies)
- **Misk Foundation, EVC** (Saudi governmental)
- **McKinsey Digital** (Digital transformation leaders)

### Their Common Hero Section Elements:

#### 1. **Headline Formula:**
```
[Action Verb] + [Specific Outcome] + [Target Audience] + [Geographic/Industry Context]
```
Examples:
- "Empowering Saudi Vision 2030 Through Digital Government Solutions"
- "Trusted Digital Transformation Partner for GCC Governments & Enterprises"
- "Building Tomorrow's Digital Infrastructure for the Middle East"

#### 2. **Subheadline Formula:**
```
[Credibility] + [Specific Service] + [Measurable Impact]
```
Keep it to 15-20 words max.

#### 3. **Visual Hierarchy:**
```
1. Trust Badge (Small, top)
2. Headline (Largest)
3. Subheadline (Medium)
4. Social Proof/Stats (Visual, scannable)
5. CTAs (Contrasting, clear)
```

#### 4. **Background Image Guidelines:**
- **70% white overlay** on left for text
- **30% visible image** on right
- Image should show: modern architecture, collaboration, or technology
- Must be regionally relevant (GCC landmarks/context)
